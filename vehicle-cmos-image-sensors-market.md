Mid-high pixel CIS (2-5M) enhances image quality for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and provides better detail for features like lane departure warnings. High-pixel CIS (5-8M) is utilized in sophisticated applications, including autonomous driving systems and high-resolution imaging for comprehensive vehicle safety.</p></p>
