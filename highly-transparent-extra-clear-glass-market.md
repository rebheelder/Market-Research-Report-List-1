<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Highly Transparent Extra Clear Glass market encompasses two primary types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a textured surface often used in architectural applications. In contrast, float glass is manufactured by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a smooth, uniform sheet ideal for windows and facades. Both types are valued for their clarity and low iron content, making them suitable for applications requiring high transparency and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
