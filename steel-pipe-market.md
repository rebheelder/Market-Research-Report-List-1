<p><p>The steel pipe market is broadly categorized into welded and seamless steel pipes. Welded steel pipes are manufactured by rolling steel plates or coils and welding the edges, making them cost-effective and suitable for low-pressure applications. Seamless steel pipes are produced by extruding solid billets, offering superior strength and durability, ideal for high-pressure and critical applications. Both types serve industries like oil & gas, construction, and automotive, with demand driven by infrastructure development and industrial growth.</p></p>
