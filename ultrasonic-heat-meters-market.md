<p><p>Ultrasonic heat meters are essential for measuring energy consumption in heating systems. The market comprises two main types: integrated heat meters and split heat meters. Integrated heat meters combine the flow sensor and temperature sensors in one unit, offering compactness and ease of installation. In contrast, split heat meters feature separate components for flow measurement and temperature sensing, allowing for flexible installation and maintenance. Both types leverage ultrasonic technology to ensure precise and reliable measurements, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
