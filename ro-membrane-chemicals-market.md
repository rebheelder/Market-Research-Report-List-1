<p><p>The RO membrane chemicals market includes **antiscalants**, **cleaning chemicals**, and **pretreatment chemicals**. Antiscalants prevent scale formation on membranes, enhancing efficiency and lifespan. Cleaning chemicals remove fouling and deposits, restoring membrane performance. Pretreatment chemicals prepare feed water by removing impurities, reducing membrane stress. These chemicals are essential for maintaining RO system functionality, optimizing water quality, and minimizing operational costs. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring the longevity and effectiveness of RO membranes in various industrial and municipal water treatment applications.</p></p>
