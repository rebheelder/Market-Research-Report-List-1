<p><ul><li>Monopole</li><li>Multipole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw-in connector market is primarily categorized into two types: monopole and multipole connectors. Monopole connectors have a single contact point, making them ideal for simple, one-to-one connections in various applications. In contrast, multipole connectors feature multiple contact points, allowing for the simultaneous connection of several circuits, which is essential in complex systems. These connectors are widely used in industries like telecommunications, automotive, and industrial machinery, where reliable and secure connections are crucial for performance and safety.</p></p>
