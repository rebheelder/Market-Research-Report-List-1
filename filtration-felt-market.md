<p><p>The Filtration Felt Market is categorized into two main types: wet and dry markets. Wet filtration felt is primarily used in applications that require the separation of solids from liquids, such as in wastewater treatment and chemical processing. It often provides superior flow rates and is resistant to moisture. In contrast, dry filtration felt is utilized in processes that involve air or gas filtration, commonly found in industrial settings to capture particulate matter. Each type serves specific operational needs and performance criteria.</p></p>
