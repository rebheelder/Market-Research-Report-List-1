<p><p>The pine honey market consists of three primary types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey is produced by removing honey from honeycomb using centrifugal force, offering a smooth product. Pressed honey is obtained by pressing the honeycomb, retaining some of the wax for texture. Comb honey, on the other hand, is honey that is sold directly in the honeycomb, providing a natural and unprocessed option with a unique presentation. Each type caters to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
