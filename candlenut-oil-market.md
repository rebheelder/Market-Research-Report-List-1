<p><p>The Candlenut Oil market is segmented into three main types based on fatty acid composition: Linoleic Oil, Mid-Oleic Oil, and High-Oleic Oil. Linoleic Oil contains a higher proportion of polyunsaturated fats, making it suitable for culinary and health applications. Mid-Oleic Oil offers a balanced fatty acid profile, appealing for both food and cosmetic uses. High-Oleic Oil, rich in monounsaturated fats, is favored for frying and longer shelf life, providing enhanced stability and nutritional benefits for consumers.</p></p>
