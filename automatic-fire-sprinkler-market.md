<p><strong>The Automatic Fire Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bubbles</li><li>Fusible Alloy</li><li>Stent Type</li><li>Heat Sink Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Fire Sprinkler market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Glass Bubbles**: These contain a colored liquid that expands and breaks the glass at a specific temperature, triggering the sprinkler.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Fusible Alloy**: Utilizes a low melting point alloy that melts and opens the sprinkler when exposed to high heat.</p><p>3. **Stent Type**: Features a heat-sensitive spring mechanism that releases sprinkler activation upon reaching a targeted temperature.</p><p>4. **Heat Sink Type**: Absorbs heat to prevent premature activation, ensuring reliability during a fire event. Each type serves crucial roles in fire safety systems.</p></p>
