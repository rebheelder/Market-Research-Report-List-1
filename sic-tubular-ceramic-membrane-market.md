<p><p>The SiC tubular ceramic membrane market is categorized into two primary types: microfiltration and ultrafiltration. Microfiltration membranes have pore sizes typically ranging from 0.1 to 10 micrometers, effectively removing suspended solids, bacteria, and larger particulate matter from liquids. In contrast, ultrafiltration membranes, with smaller pore sizes of 1 nanometer to 0.1 micrometers, can separate smaller molecules, including proteins and polysaccharides. Both technologies are utilized across various industries, including water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals, enhancing purification and separation processes.</p></p>
