<p><p>The 6-inch silicon carbide substrate market is categorized into two primary types based on conductivity: conductive and semi-insulated. Conductive substrates are ideal for high-power applications, enabling efficient electron flow and thermal management, making them suitable for use in power electronics and electric vehicles. Semi-insulated substrates, on the other hand, provide a balance between conductivity and insulation, offering improved performance for radio frequency and high-temperature applications. Both types play a crucial role in advancing semiconductor technologies and enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
