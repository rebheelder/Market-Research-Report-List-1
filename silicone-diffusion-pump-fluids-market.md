<p><p>Silicone diffusion pump fluids are essential for high-vacuum applications, primarily categorized into low and high viscosity silicone oils. Low viscosity silicone oils are characterized by their fluidity, providing efficient vaporization and rapid pumping speeds, ideal for applications requiring quick evacuation of gases. In contrast, high viscosity silicone oils offer superior thermal stability and reduced backstreaming, making them suitable for processes where contamination control is critical. Both types serve distinct industries, ensuring optimal performance depending on specific operational needs.</p></p>
