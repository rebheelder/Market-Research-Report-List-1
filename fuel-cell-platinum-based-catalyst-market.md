<p><ul><li>Pt Alloy Catalyst</li><li>Pt/C Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel cell platinum-based catalyst market is primarily divided into two types: Pt alloy catalysts and Pt/C catalysts. Pt alloy catalysts enhance performance by mixing platinum with other metals, improving efficiency and reducing costs. These alloys often exhibit better resistance to degradation and carbon monoxide poisoning. Pt/C catalysts, on the other hand, consist of platinum nanoparticles supported on carbon, offering high catalytic activity and effective conductivity. Both types play crucial roles in advancing fuel cell technology for various applications, including transportation and stationary power.</p></p>
