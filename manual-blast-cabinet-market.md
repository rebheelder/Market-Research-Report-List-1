<p><strong>The Manual Blast Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Blast Cabinet</li><li>Suction Blast Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual blast cabinets are designed for abrasive blasting, with two primary types: pressure blast cabinets and suction blast cabinets. Pressure blast cabinets utilize a pressurized system to propel abrasive materials forcefully, ensuring faster and more efficient surface cleaning and finishing. In contrast, suction blast cabinets rely on a suction mechanism to draw abrasives from a reservoir, offering more control over the blast intensity. Both types cater to various applications, including metalworking, automotive, and restoration, enhancing productivity and precision in surface treatment.</p></p>
