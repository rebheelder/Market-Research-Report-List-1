<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) ceramic membranes are crucial in various filtration applications, characterized by two main types: pipe filter membranes and plate filter membranes. Pipe filter membranes are cylindrical and designed for high flow rates, making them suitable for industrial wastewater treatment and oil separation. In contrast, plate filter membranes have a flat structure that offers a larger surface area, ideal for applications requiring efficient filtration in confined spaces. Both types leverage SiC's durability and chemical resistance, enhancing performance in harsh environments.</p></p>
